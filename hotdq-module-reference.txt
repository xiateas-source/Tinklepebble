HOARD OF THE DRAGON QUEEN — QUICK REFERENCE

CULT RANKS: Initiate → Dragonclaw (1st) → Dragonwing (2nd) → Dragonfang (3rd) → Dragonsoul (4th) → Wearer of Purple → Wyrmspeaker
CULT SIGN: Right hand extended, palm forward, all five fingers spread (Tiamat's five heads)
CULT REGALIA: Black clothing, cape/cloak cut like dragon wings, draconic jewelry. Not worn in public.

WYRMSPEAKERS (Inner Circle):
- Severin Silrajin — Red Wyrmspeaker, cult leader (Calishite human)

- Rezmir — Black Wyrmspeaker (female half-black dragon)
- Varram — White Wyrmspeaker (male dwarf, skeptical of Red Wizard alliance)

DRAGON MASKS: Five chromatic masks (red, blue, green, black, white). Combined = Mask of the Dragon Queen. Opens portal to free Tiamat from Nine Hells.

DRAAKHORN: Ancient draconic artifact summoning chromatic dragons to the cult's cause.

MAGIC ITEMS: Hazirawn (greatsword), Dragontooth Dagger, Insignia of Claws, Wand of Winter, Tankard of Plenty

MILESTONE LEVELING: Level up after each chapter EXCEPT Chapter 5.
