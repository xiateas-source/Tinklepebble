📐 Conditions Reference
Blinded: Auto-fail sight checks, attack rolls vs you have adv.
Charmed: Can't attack charmer, charmer has adv on social checks.
Exhaustion: Levels 1–6, cumulative penalties to ability checks.
Frightened: Disadv on checks/attacks while source is visible.
Grappled: Speed 0, ends if grappler is incapacitated.
Incapacitated: No actions or reactions.
Invisible: Heavily obscured, adv on attacks, disadv vs you.
Paralyzed: Incap, auto-fail Str/Dex saves, hits within 5ft crit.
Petrified: Transformed to stone, incap, resist all damage.
Poisoned: Disadv on attack rolls and ability checks.
Prone: Disadv on attacks, adv on attacks vs from 5ft.
Restrained: Speed 0, disadv on attacks, adv on attacks vs you.
Stunned: Incap, auto-fail Str/Dex saves, adv on attacks vs you.
Unconscious: Incap, prone, auto-fail Str/Dex, hits from 5ft crit.
