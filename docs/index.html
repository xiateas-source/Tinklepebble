🔔 Context Refresh — use first

Sends the current scene to the AI — location, conditions, who's present. Use when the AI loses the thread or contradicts something recent. Think: tap the DM on the shoulder.

Use when: AI forgot what room you're in · AI described wrong weather · AI called a character by wrong name once

Sends the full compiled ledger — every PC stat, quest, NPC, inventory, treasury. Use when the AI is meaningfully wrong about persistent state or at session start. Think: hand the DM all their notes.

Use when: AI has wrong HP · Starting a new session · AI drifted significantly from reality
